# Community Standards & Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We — contributors and maintainers of the Touhou 6 macOS/Web reimplementation —
pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for
everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity
and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race,
religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We are a small fan project built out of respect for ZUN's work. We expect that
respect to extend to each other.

## Our Standards

Behavior that helps build a welcoming community:

- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback on code and design.
- Focusing on what is best for the project and the wider Touhou community.
- Showing empathy toward other contributors and players.

Behavior that is not acceptable:

- Harassment, insults, derogatory comments, or personal/political attacks.
- Sexualized language or imagery, or unwelcome sexual attention.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.
- Sustained disruption of discussions, issues, or pull requests.

## Project-Specific Rules

This project is a clean-room engine reimplementation. The following are
**hard requirements**, not preferences:

1. **No copyrighted asset files.** Do not commit, attach, link, or request
   game *data files* — `.DAT` archives, ripped sprite/tile sheets, BGM/SFX
   audio files, ROMs, or the original executables. This repository contains
   code only; anything that lets someone reconstruct game data will be removed.

   Screenshots and short gameplay clips for bug reports or discussion are
   fine — they are fan content, not redistributable game data.

2. **Bring your own copy.** All discussion assumes users legally own
   *Touhou Koumakyou ~ the Embodiment of Scarlet Devil*. Do not ask where to
   pirate the game, and do not provide such links.

3. **Respect ZUN and Team Shanghai Alice.** This is an unofficial fan project.
   We follow the spirit of ZUN's [guidelines for derivative
   works](https://en.touhouwiki.net/wiki/Touhou_Project#Doujin_culture).
   Nothing here is for sale.

4. **Keep it non-commercial.** Do not use this project, its name, or its code
   to sell products or run paid services.

## Scope

These standards apply in all project spaces — the repository, issues, pull
requests, discussions — and in public spaces when an individual is
representing the project.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior, or copyright/piracy violations, by opening a
[private security advisory](https://github.com/TheScreamer74/Touhou6/security/advisories/new)
or contacting the maintainer directly via their GitHub profile
([@TheScreamer74](https://github.com/TheScreamer74)).

Maintainers will review and respond to every report. They may remove, edit, or
reject comments, commits, code, issues, and contributions that violate these
standards, and may temporarily or permanently ban any contributor for
behavior they deem inappropriate, threatening, or harmful.
